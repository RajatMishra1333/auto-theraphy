Why Ceramic Coating is Worth It

  1st April 2026/   Auto Therapy/   Ceramic Coating

Ceramic coating is one of the best long-term investments you can make for your vehicle. Unlike traditional wax, a 9H ceramic coating bonds chemically with your car's paint, providing a permanent hydrophobic layer that repels water, dust, bird droppings, and UV rays. PPF vs Ceramic Coating – What's Right for You?

  20th March 2026/   Auto Therapy/   Paint Protection

Paint Protection Film (PPF) and Ceramic Coating both protect your vehicle, but in different ways. PPF is a thick self-healing TPU film that physically shields paint from rock chips and scratches, while ceramic coating provides a chemical barrier against contamination and UV damage. Many of our customers in Ludhiana opt for both for maximum protection — and we offer combined packages to suit every budget.
